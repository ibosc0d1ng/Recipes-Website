Lasagna

Description

The easiest and quickest Lasagna recipe! This recipe will have your family surprised at your cooking skills.

Lasagna is filling while being not too heavy and is perfect for both a solo and family dinner!

  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 4 Cups Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/2 lb Ground Beef
  • 1/2 lb Italian Sausage Optional
  • 1 Onion Diced
  • 2 Cloves Garlic Minced
  • 36 oz Pasta Sauce
  • 2 Tbs Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 2 Cups Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Egg Beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Cook pasta until it is al dente or according to package instructions
  2. Cook beef, onion, garlic and onion until meat is brown and no pink is left
  3. Drain any grease
  4. Stir pasta, tomate paste and Italian Seasoning and let simmer for around 5 minutes
  5. Make the cheese mixture. In a seperate bowl, mix 1 and a half cups Mozzarella, 1/4 cup Parmesan, Ricotta, and egg
  6. Add 1 cup of meat to a flat pan around size 10x14
  7. Add 3 lasagna noodles on top, then add 1/3 cup of the cheese mixture, then lastly 1 cup of meat sauce. Repeat this process 2 more times and top with 3 lasagna noodles at the top with remaining sauce
  8. Cover pan with foil and bake for 45 minutes
  9. Once the 45 minutes are over, remove the pan from the oven, sprinkle the rest of the cheese on top and bake for another 15 minutes
  10. Broil for 3-5 minutes
  11. Remove the pan from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before cutting.
