Pancakes

Description

The fluffiest pancakes ever! These pancakes will melt in your mouth. This recipe is super easy to make and will make you want to make pancakes every morning.

These pancakes are not too sweet and you only need a few ingredients to make this recipe. Most of the ingredients required are most likely already in your kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 1 1/4 Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 4 Tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Firstly, whisk flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a medium bowl
  2. Warm the milk until it is about lukewarm. If it is too hot, let it cool
  3. In the same bowl, add the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract and whisk until the mixture is combined.
  4. Heat a griddle or a skillet to medium heat
  5. Brush some butter on the griddle or skillet and add a little batter to the skillet
  6. Spread the batter evenly across the skillet or the griddle
  7. When the edges are close to dry, try to flip the pancake. If the bottom is still sticky, let it cook more. If not, flip until both sides are golden brown.
  8. Serve with syrup, butter and fruits
  9. Enjoy!!
