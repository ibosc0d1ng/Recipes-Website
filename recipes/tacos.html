Ground Beef Tacos

Description

The best ground beef Tacos EVER! This easy homemade recipe will get you addicted to Tacos.

These Tacos can be prepared in less than half an hour and it feeds multiple people!

Ingredients

  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • Butter
  • Ground Beef
  • Tortillas
  • Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Chili Powder

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef on medium-high heat until it's fully brown
  2. Drain the grease
  3. Lower heat and add the seasoning and some tomato sauce
  4. Let the meat simmer for some time in the sauce for more flavor
  5. Fry the tortillas in oil in a skillet on medium-low heat
  6. Once both sides are fried, fold them in half to give it it's shape
  7. Place meat in the tortillas and add any desired sauce
